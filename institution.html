Beyond The Publication
The media is how people know we exist.
The institution is what we actually are.

Docs Africa isn't a media company that happens to run events. It is a cultural institution that uses media as evidence of its deeper work — discovering builders, making their stories permanent, and convening the community that grows around that discovery.

01
Award Nights

The ceremony where discovery becomes official. Documented builders gather. The world acknowledges what was always true. The award night is not a celebration — it is an induction.

  • Annual ceremony
  • Builders, partners, press
  • The night the archive comes alive
02
Builder Network

Every builder Docs Africa documents becomes part of a community of documented builders. They refer each other. They show up for each other. Being discovered by Docs Africa means belonging to something.

  • Private network of documented builders
  • Cross-referrals and collaboration
  • The alumni effect
03
Partnerships

Brands, investors, and institutions that want to be associated with the discovery. Not advertising. Association. The difference between a sponsor and a believer.

  • Brand partnerships
  • Commissioned profiles
  • Institutional collaborations
04
The Discovery

At some point, organizations will want Docs Africa to find builders for them. Accelerators, VCs, development bodies. The discovery as a service — but on our terms, with our standards, at our pace.

  • Curated discovery for partners
  • On Docs Africa's terms only
  • The scout, not the directory
